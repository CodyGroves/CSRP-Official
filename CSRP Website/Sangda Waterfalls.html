Within CSRP we have many different departments that our members can join. First, as every server has we have our LEO departments, which includes The Los Angeles Sheriff's Office, California Highway Patrol and the just opened Los Angeles Police Department.

Then to make the RP better and more interesting within the server, we have Cal Fire, which includes Fire/EMS along with there many sub-departments. ​ Furthermore, we have a few more departments to add that little bit more to our member's RP experience. We have a whitelisted civilian division and FAA to give members the permission to fly helicopters and planes within the server. ​ Making our members have a wide choice of interesting and different departments to choose from is something that is important to us.

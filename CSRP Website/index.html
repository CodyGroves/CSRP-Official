Overview

Welcome to California State Roleplay! We are a Xbox one/FiveM roleplay server with over 700 members. We have daily roleplays and tons of Giveaways such as Department promotions and much more!

We have many different departments to choose from such as California State Police // Los Angeles Sheriffs office // Fire and EMS // Communications // and Civilian.

How can you benefit from joining CSRP?

You can have a chance at winning one of many giveaways hosted by our amazing staff team and Executive team. You can have fun and create fun and interactive scenes with other members, You can put your creativity to use by coming up with scenes and so much more!!
